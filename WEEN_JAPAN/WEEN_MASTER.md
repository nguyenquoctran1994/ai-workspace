# WEEN Master

Project-wide strategy and master plan for WEEN JAPAN.

## Brand Positioning
- WEEN JAPAN is a hybrid wellness growth support system for teenagers.
- Primary focus: wellness growth-support system.
- Secondary focus: teen growth coaching ecosystem, Japanese-inspired biotech lifestyle, premium wellness habit tracking.
- WEEN is not a medical clinic, miracle height promise, gym supplement brand, or childish teen app.
- Core idea: guide teenagers to build daily habits for sleep, recovery, movement, nutrition awareness, consistency, and progress tracking.

## Audience
- Decision maker: parents.
- Emotional user: teenagers.
- Voice: calm, supportive, trustworthy, educational, disciplined, modern, parent-friendly.
- Avoid hype, fear-based pressure, fake urgency, exaggerated claims, and aggressive supplement selling.

## Offer & Business Model
- The product is a combined system: supplement packs, sleep and recovery awareness, coaching, tracking, accountability, and motivation.
- The real offering is a guided daily growth-supporting system, not just supplement packs.
- Website goals: collect leads, explain the system, recommend packs, sell coaching/tracking, and build a trust ecosystem.
- Recommended flow: content → landing page → lead form → consultation → pack recommendation → tracking → AI follow-up → retention.

## AI / Codex Guidance
- Read the WEEN brand DNA, product logic, UI design, and tracking rules before editing.
- Keep work mobile-first, reusable, simple, and beginner-friendly.
- Avoid unnecessary packages, medical claims, miracle height claims, and fake urgency.
- Prefer clear, concise edits and preserve existing architecture when possible.
