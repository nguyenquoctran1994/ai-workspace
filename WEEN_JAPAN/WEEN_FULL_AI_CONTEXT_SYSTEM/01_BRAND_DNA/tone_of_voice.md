# TONE OF VOICE

WEEN should speak in a calm premium wellness tone.

Voice:
- calm
- supportive
- trustworthy
- educational
- disciplined
- parent-friendly
- modern

Avoid:
- hype
- fear-based pressure
- fake urgency
- exaggerated claims
- aggressive supplement selling

Preferred message:
“A guided growth-supporting habit system.”

Avoid message:
“Use this and you will become taller.”
