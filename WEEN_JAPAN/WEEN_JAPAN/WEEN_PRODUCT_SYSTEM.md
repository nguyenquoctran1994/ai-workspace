# WEEN Product System

Product system definitions and structure for WEEN JAPAN.

## Product Logic
- WEEN sells a combined system: supplement packs, sleep and recovery awareness, coaching, tracking, accountability, and community motivation.
- The real product is a guided daily growth-supporting system, not just supplement packs.

## Product Pillars
- Sleep
- Recovery
- Movement
- Nutrition habit
- Consistency
- Coaching
- Progress tracking

## Offer Structure
- Present clear pack recommendations with optional coaching and tracking add-ons.
- Keep bundle logic simple and premium: packs, system support, and accountability.
- Pricing should be transparent, easy to compare, and support straightforward choices.

## Usage Positioning
- Position WEEN as a daily routine system: check in on height, sleep, exercise, supplements, and notes.
- Encourage simple tracking, streaks, progress badges, weekly score, and milestone celebration.
- Avoid overpromising height growth or miracle claims.

## Website Goals
- Collect leads.
- Sell packs and coaching/tracking support.
- Build parent trust with a calm premium system.
- Prioritize simple check-in, AI follow-up, and lightweight gamification for MVP.
