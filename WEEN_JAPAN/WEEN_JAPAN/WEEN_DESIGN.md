# WEEN Design

Design system notes and branding direction for WEEN JAPAN.

## Brand Identity
- Premium Japanese-inspired wellness with a calm biotech aesthetic.
- Not a bodybuilding supplement brand, medical/pharmacy brand, or loud dropshipping/gaming style.
- Core emotional positioning: trustworthy, premium, modern, achievable, and supportive.

## Visual System
- Use a dual-layer system: a traffic layer for teen engagement and a core brand layer for parent trust.
- Traffic visual language should feel energetic, aspirational, cinematic, and modern youth-focused.
- Core brand language should feel calm, premium, minimal, trustworthy, and biotech-inspired.

## Colors
- Primary: deep navy, midnight blue, dark indigo.
- Accent: electric cyan, soft purple glow, neon blue highlights.
- Optional: soft pink-purple gradients and cinematic white glow.
- Avoid oversaturated rainbow colors, hard red gym tones, crypto/neon gaming aesthetics, and hospital UI.

## Typography
- Use modern, sharp, clean typography with strong hierarchy.
- Bold headlines and large hook text help emotional engagement.
- Avoid overly corporate fonts, childish rounded fonts, and cluttered typography stacks.

## Packaging Style
- Packaging should feel premium Japanese wellness, modern nutraceutical, and organized for daily use.
- It must feel trustworthy to parents and exciting to teenagers.
- Avoid hardcore supplement branding, bodybuilding aesthetics, fake pharmaceutical design, and loud ecommerce visuals.

## Social Content Style
- Social visuals should educate first, emotionally motivate second, and sell indirectly.
- Keep layouts spacious, modular, and high readability with rounded cards and clean spacing.
- Avoid fear-based manipulation, exaggerated promises, spammy urgency, and fake medical claims.

## Component Style
- Reuse core components such as HeroSection, TrackingCard, PackCard, ProgressCard, and CTASection.
- Prefer rounded corners (24–32px), soft shadows, clean hierarchy, and mobile-first layouts.
