# WEEN Content System

Content strategy, messaging, and marketing systems for WEEN JAPAN.

## Content Pillars
- Sleep habits
- Growth-supporting routines
- Exercise consistency
- Posture awareness
- Nutrition basics
- Parent education
- Teen motivation
- Tracking and accountability

## Tone
- Calm
- Premium
- Supportive
- Trustworthy
- Educational
- Disciplined
- Parent-friendly
- Vietnamese posts should remain respectful, family-oriented, and not hype-driven.

## Caption Rules
- Educate first, motivate second.
- Sell indirectly and keep messages simple.
- Use positive, aspirational language without exaggeration.
- Avoid hype, fear, fake urgency, and miracle-height claims.

## Forbidden Content
Never use:
- anime style
- bodybuilding visual language
- fake doctor imagery
- before-after scam visuals
- hospital UI
- neon gaming overload
- miracle height claims
- guaranteed height increase claims
- medical diagnosis wording

## Social Content Direction
- Build trust before selling.
- Combine calm educational content with viral motivational and premium lifestyle cues.
- Focus on habits, routines, growth mindset, and self-improvement.
