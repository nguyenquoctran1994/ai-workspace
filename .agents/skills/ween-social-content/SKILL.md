# WEEN JAPAN Social Content Skill

## Purpose

Use this skill to create consistent, compliant, premium social content for WEEN JAPAN.

This skill is for content direction, captions, post concepts, carousel scripts, social image prompts, and daily content planning.

Do not duplicate the full brand system here. Always read the required project files when the task needs brand accuracy.

## When to use this skill

Use this skill when the user asks for:

- WEEN JAPAN social media posts
- Vietnamese captions
- Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, or Zalo content
- Carousel post content
- Daily content ideas
- Content calendars
- Image prompts for WEEN JAPAN posts
- Educational wellness content
- Growth-support habit content
- Parent/teen communication content
- Product-support content that must avoid exaggerated medical claims
- Review of wording for compliance or social platform safety

  Use `chillab-daily-content` instead.

- HUONG fashion content

## Core brand direction

WEEN JAPAN should feel like:

- Premium Japanese wellness lifestyle
- Modern discipline and daily routine
- Growth-support ecosystem
- Calm, clean, scientific, and trustworthy
- Parent-friendly but still emotionally relatable for teenagers
- Educational first, product second
- Supportive, not fear-based
- Premium but not cold
- Vietnamese-first communication

WEEN JAPAN is not:

- A medicine
- A pharmacy-style advertisement
- A miracle height-growth product
- A body-shaming brand
- A spammy supplement page
- A loud hard-selling brand

## Vietnamese-first content rules

Default language: Vietnamese.

Use English only when:

- The user requests bilingual content
- The phrase is a short premium brand phrase
- The English phrase already belongs to the WEEN JAPAN design system

Vietnamese tone should be:

- Clear
- Warm
- Confident
- Educational
- Modern
- Not overly submissive
- Not overly salesy
- Not filled with excessive honorifics

Avoid weak or overly humble phrasing such as:

- “Dạ thưa”
- “Em xin phép”
- “Bé yêu”
- “Cam kết cao lên”
- “Chắc chắn hiệu quả”
- “Uống là cao”

Preferred wording examples:

- “Hỗ trợ”
- “Đồng hành”
- “Xây dựng thói quen”
- “Theo dõi đều đặn”
- “Nền tảng phát triển”
- “Giấc ngủ, vận động và dinh dưỡng”
- “Phù hợp để tham khảo trong hành trình chăm sóc chiều cao”

## Compliance and safety wording rules

Never claim or imply:

- Guaranteed height increase
- Cure, treatment, or medical correction
- Reversal of puberty or bone growth limits
- Product alone can determine height
- Exact growth results such as “cao thêm X cm”
- Fear-based messaging that pressures parents or teenagers
- Body shaming toward short teenagers
- Comparison that makes a teen feel inferior

Avoid high-risk phrases:

- “Tăng chiều cao chắc chắn”
- “Cam kết cao”
- “Hiệu quả 100%”
- “Thay đổi chiều cao nhanh chóng”
- “Điều trị thấp còi”
- “Chữa chậm lớn”
- “Kích thích xương phát triển thần tốc”
- “Không dùng là bỏ lỡ cơ hội”

Use safer alternatives:

- “Hỗ trợ nền tảng phát triển chiều cao”
- “Đồng hành cùng thói quen ngủ, vận động và dinh dưỡng”
- “Giúp hành trình chăm sóc chiều cao có hệ thống hơn”
- “Theo dõi tiến trình để điều chỉnh thói quen mỗi ngày”
- “Không thay thế tư vấn từ chuyên gia y tế khi có vấn đề sức khỏe”

For teen-related content, keep the tone supportive and avoid making physical appearance the main source of self-worth.

## Content workflow

Follow this workflow for most WEEN JAPAN social content tasks:

### 1. Identify the content goal

Choose one primary goal:

- Educate
- Build trust
- Start conversation
- Collect customer information
- Explain routine
- Introduce product system softly
- Promote offer softly
- Handle objection
- Drive comment or DM

### 2. Identify target audience

Choose the audience:

- Parent researching for child
- Teen user
- Student
- Existing customer
- New lead
- Buyer comparing options
- Community follower

### 3. Choose content angle

Use one clear angle per post:

- Sleep and recovery
- Posture and screen-time lifestyle
- Nutrition habits
- Exercise and movement
- Routine discipline
- Growth tracking
- Parent guidance
- Teen motivation
- Product system explanation
- Myth versus fact
- Checklist
- Before/after habit awareness, not body transformation

### 4. Create the output

Match the requested format:

- Caption
- Carousel script
- Image prompt
- Daily post concept
- Content calendar
- DM message
- Comment reply
- Compliance review
- Creative direction

### 5. Final quality check

Before finalizing, check:

- Vietnamese sounds natural
- No exaggerated growth claims
- No medical treatment claims
- No body shaming
- Product is positioned as support, not magic
- Design direction follows WEEN JAPAN
- CTA is soft and community-first
- Content is useful even without buying

## Image prompt rules

When creating image prompts:

- Always protect the official WEEN JAPAN logo.
- Never ask the image model to invent a new logo.
- Use “official attached logo” when the user has provided a logo.
- Vietnamese text must be short to reduce text errors.
- Prefer large headline, small support line, and clean composition.
- Avoid crowded layouts.
- Avoid hospital, pharmacy, disease, or medicine visual language.
- Avoid unrealistic body transformation visuals.
- Avoid before/after height comparison that shames the shorter person.
- Use lifestyle scenes, routines, study spaces, sleep, nutrition, posture awareness, and tracking systems.

Preferred visual style:

- Premium Japanese biotech wellness
- Deep navy background
- Cyan glow accents
- Clean scientific layout
- Soft product shadows
- Minimal typography
- Calm cinematic lighting
- Apple-style spacing
- Trustworthy, modern, parent-friendly

## Do rules
