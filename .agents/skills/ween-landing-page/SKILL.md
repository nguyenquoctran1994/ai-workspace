# WEEN JAPAN Landing Page Skill

## Purpose

Use this skill to build and improve the WEEN JAPAN landing page as a premium Vietnamese-first wellness growth-support experience.

The landing page should explain the WEEN JAPAN system clearly, build trust with parents, emotionally connect with teenagers, and convert visitors into leads or customers without using exaggerated medical or height-growth claims.

Do not duplicate the full WEEN brand documents inside the skill output. Read and reference the project files only as needed.

Use this skill when the user asks for:

- WEEN JAPAN landing page structure
- Landing page copywriting

## Landing page goal hierarchy

Prioritize goals in this order unless the user says otherwise:

1. Build trust
2. Explain the WEEN JAPAN system
3. Collect lead information
4. Guide the user to the right routine or pack
5. Softly support purchase decision
6. Encourage tracking, coaching, or community participation

Do not make the page feel like a hard-selling supplement page.

## Core audience logic

### Parent audience

Parents are usually the decision maker. They need:

- Trust
- Safety
- Clear explanation
- Reasonable expectations
- Product and habit-system logic
- Proof of seriousness
- Easy next step

### Teen audience

Teenagers are usually the emotional user. They need:

- Motivation
- Confidence
- Friendly tone
- Habit tracking
- Routine feeling
- A system that feels modern, not medical

The page should speak mostly to parents but include emotional cues for teenagers.

## Brand and visual direction

The landing page should feel like:

- Premium Japanese biotech wellness
- Calm, scientific, and modern
- Clean Apple-style layout
- Deep navy / biotech blue color system
- Soft cyan glow accents
- White space and modular cards
- Premium lifestyle, not pharmacy
- Trustworthy and Vietnamese-first

Use official WEEN JAPAN logo/assets only when available. Do not recreate or redesign the logo.

## Recommended page structure

Default landing page flow:

1. Hero section
   - Main positioning
   - Soft promise
   - Primary CTA
   - Secondary CTA
   - Premium visual or product-system visual

2. Problem insight
   - Explain that height-support is not only about buying supplements
   - Introduce routine gaps: sleep, nutrition, movement, posture, tracking

3. WEEN JAPAN system
   - Present WEEN as a daily growth-support ecosystem
   - Connect product, habit, tracking, and coaching

4. Growth foundation pillars
   - Sleep and recovery
   - Nutrition foundation
   - Movement and posture
   - Consistency and tracking

5. Product / pack overview
   - Explain product roles safely
   - Do not promise direct height increase
   - Keep copy benefit-led but compliant

6. Tracking / consultation flow
   - Explain how customer information is collected
   - Show how WEEN recommends a suitable routine

7. Trust section
   - Brand philosophy
   - Ingredient transparency if available
   - Usage guidance
   - Safety note
   - Parent-friendly reassurance

8. Social proof / education assets
   - Use real posts, guides, checklists, or resources when available
   - Do not create fake testimonials

9. FAQ
   - Address common objections and safety questions
   - Use careful wording

10. Final CTA
   - Soft conversion
   - Lead form, Zalo, Messenger, or consultation CTA

## Copywriting rules

Default language: Vietnamese.

Use English only for short brand phrases or if requested.

Preferred tone:

- Clear
- Calm
- Premium
- Educational
- Trustworthy
- Supportive
- Not overly submissive
- Not aggressive

Use words like:

- “hỗ trợ”
- “đồng hành”
- “nền tảng”
- “thói quen”
- “theo dõi”
- “routine”
- “hệ thống”
- “phù hợp để tham khảo”

Avoid words like:

- “cam kết cao”
- “đảm bảo tăng chiều cao”
- “cao nhanh”
- “trị thấp còi”
- “chữa chậm lớn”
- “kích thích tăng trưởng thần tốc”
- “uống là cao”

## Compliance and safety wording rules

Never claim or imply:

- Guaranteed height increase
- Exact cm growth results
- Cure or treatment of medical conditions
- Product alone determines height
- A routine can reopen growth plates
- A teen is inferior because of height
- Fear-based pressure on parents

Use safer wording:

- “Hỗ trợ nền tảng phát triển chiều cao”
- “Đồng hành cùng giấc ngủ, dinh dưỡng, vận động và theo dõi thói quen”
- “Giúp hành trình chăm sóc chiều cao có hệ thống hơn”
- “Không thay thế tư vấn từ chuyên gia y tế khi có vấn đề sức khỏe”
- “Kết quả phụ thuộc vào nhiều yếu tố như độ tuổi, di truyền, giấc ngủ, dinh dưỡng, vận động và mức độ duy trì routine”

For users under 18, keep the page supportive and avoid body dissatisfaction framing.

## Lead form rules

A WEEN landing page lead form should collect only necessary information.

Recommended fields:

- Người dùng là phụ huynh hay học sinh
- Tuổi
- Giới tính
- Chiều cao hiện tại
- Cân nặng hiện tại
- Chiều cao của ba và mẹ, if appropriate
- Dậy thì hay chưa, phrased carefully
- Thói quen ngủ
- Thói quen vận động
- Mục tiêu hoặc vấn đề đang quan tâm
- Contact channel: phone, Zalo, Messenger, or email

Use privacy-friendly wording. Do not ask for sensitive medical details unless the user explicitly needs consultation logic and the project files support it.

## CTA rules

Use soft CTA language:

- “Nhận tư vấn routine phù hợp”
- “Kiểm tra nền tảng phát triển hiện tại”
- “Nhận checklist theo dõi miễn phí”
- “Bắt đầu đánh giá thói quen”
- “Inbox WEEN để được hướng dẫn”

Avoid hard CTA language:

- “Mua ngay để cao hơn”
- “Đừng bỏ lỡ cơ hội cuối cùng”
- “Cam kết hiệu quả”
- “Đặt hàng ngay kẻo muộn”
