# CHILLAB Daily Content Skill

## Purpose

Use this skill to create practical, local, conversion-oriented content for CHILLAB.

CHILLAB content should bring people to the venue, encourage return visits, explain simple experiences clearly, and make the bar feel alive, social, cozy, and worth visiting.

Do not make CHILLAB look like a fantasy EDM festival, Vegas club, or generic cyberpunk nightclub. Use the real CHILLAB background and local Vietnamese nightlife context whenever possible.

## When to use this skill

Use this skill when the user asks for:

- Daily CHILLAB content ideas
- Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, or story captions
- Event posts
- Weekly content calendars
- Ladies night / DJ night / open deck content
- Drinking game content
- Birthday or group booking content
- Cocktail, beer, or sharing drink promotion
- Local customer return campaigns
- Staff-friendly scripts for customer interaction
- Image prompts for CHILLAB posts or posters
- Review of CHILLAB content direction
- CHILLAB vibe, music, menu, or nightlife communication

## When not to use this skill

Do not use this skill for:

- WEEN JAPAN wellness content.
- HUONG fashion content.
- Technical DJ controller troubleshooting.
- Full menu cost calculation.
- Legal or accounting documents.
- Generic nightclub content unrelated to CHILLAB.

## Current CHILLAB business background

Use this background when helpful:

- CHILLAB is a local bar/lounge in Mỹ Tho.
- The main growth issue is not only awareness, but also weak customer return and unclear concept.
- Content should help customers understand why they should visit tonight or this week.
- Saturday is usually the strongest day.
- Direct customer messaging and simple reasons to show up often work better than abstract events.
- CHILLAB should feel local, real, social, and easy to join.

## Brand and visual direction

CHILLAB visual content should feel like:

- Real CHILLAB venue atmosphere
- Deep purple / blue / red ambient lighting
- Cozy lounge seating
- Reflective tables
- Neon accents
- Realistic smartphone-photo energy
- Candid customer moments
- VIP sofa areas
- DJ booth energy
- Crowded but intimate nightlife
- Stylish Vietnamese / Asian nightlife fashion
- Cinematic but grounded realism

Avoid:

- Fake giant festival stages
- Western Vegas nightclub fantasy
- Overly futuristic cyberpunk visuals
- Unrealistic EDM crowd scenes
- Generic AI-looking club backgrounds
- Too much fake smoke, lasers, and impossible architecture

When creating image prompts, reference real CHILLAB photos or assets if available.

## Content strategy principles

Prioritize:

1. Clear reason to visit
2. Local relatability
3. Social proof and atmosphere
4. Simple group-friendly offers
5. Repeatable weekly habits
6. Staff-executable content
7. Short, direct Vietnamese copy
8. Visual realism

Do not make every post a discount post. Rotate between vibe, social proof, menu, music, games, birthday/group booking, staff moments, and simple event hooks.

## Vietnamese voice rules

Default language: Vietnamese.

Tone should be:

- Friendly
- Local
- Natural
- Confident
- Slightly playful
- Not too formal
- Not too cringe
- Easy for staff/customers to understand

Avoid:

- Overly luxury wording that does not fit local bar context
- Long abstract copy
- Too much English
- Hard-selling every sentence
- Fake viral slang if it sounds forced

## Caption workflow

For each post, identify:

1. What reason does the customer have to come?
2. Who is the post for?
3. What feeling should the image create?
4. What action should the customer take?
5. Can staff actually execute this offer or message tonight?

## CTA rules

Use simple CTAs:

- “Rủ team ghé CHILLAB tối nay.”
- “Inbox để giữ bàn.”
- “Đi nhóm thì nhắn CHILLAB xếp chỗ đẹp.”
- “Sinh nhật / tụ họp thì nhắn trước để team chuẩn bị.”
- “Lưu lịch cuối tuần này lại.”

Avoid:

- Overhyped scarcity
- Fake urgency
- Too many emojis
- Long policy-like CTAs

## Image prompt rules

When writing CHILLAB image prompts:

- Use real CHILLAB visual background if available.
- Preserve actual CHILLAB logo if attached.
- Do not recreate or distort the logo.
- Keep Vietnamese text short and clear.
- Make the scene feel local and real.
- Use cinematic realism, not fantasy club art.
- If AI-generated crowd/background looks fake, instruct to darken, blur, or reduce detail.
- Prioritize mood, lighting, table reflections, drinks, hands, friends, DJ, and sofa areas.

Preferred prompt structure:

```md
Create a realistic CHILLAB nightlife social media visual.

Format:
- [Instagram 4:5 / Facebook post / story / cover]

Scene:
- Realistic local Vietnamese lounge bar atmosphere
- Deep purple, blue, and red ambient lighting
- Cozy sofa seating, reflective tables, neon accents
- Candid group energy, not fake festival crowd

Brand:
- Use the official CHILLAB logo only if provided
- Do not redesign or recreate the logo

Text overlay:
- Main headline: "[Vietnamese headline]"
- Supporting line: "[short line]"

Rules:
- Vietnamese text must be correct
- Keep crowd/background realistic, darker or slightly blurred if AI-generated
- Avoid Vegas, cyberpunk, or EDM festival style
```
